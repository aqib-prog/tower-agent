REGIONAL POLICIES - TELECOM TOWER LEASE REGULATIONS

DXB-North Zone:
- Maximum equipment height allowed on any structure is 45 meters.
- No single tenant asset may exceed 100kg in weight.

SHJ-South Zone:
- Due to the ocean, no single tenant asset may exceed 15kg.
- Maximum equipment height allowed on any structure is 30 meters.

SHJ-Coastal Zone:
- Due to wind-load factors near the ocean, no single tenant asset may exceed 25kg.
- Maximum equipment height allowed on any structure is 35 meters.
